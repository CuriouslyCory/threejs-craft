## Goal / scope
First player-visible slice: a signed-in player lands back in the world they built, and every
successful break/place auto-saves off the render path. On entry the client loads the user's
`{seed, deltas}` from the server, regenerates the base and hydrates the deltas before play.
Signed-out visitors keep today's zero-friction ephemeral demo (literal seed, no persistence) â€”
auth is detected by `world.load` returning `UNAUTHORIZED` (play-first soft gate).

Out of scope (unchanged): inventory + player-position persistence (auto-load restores **builds
only**); the pause-menu UI / Save / New Game (that's #21, though #20 must shape the outer
component so #21 can wire those in cleanly).
